About Me

I am a hospitality industry leader who turned into a UX/UI researcher and a bit of a designer, based in Portland, OR. My passion is to capture user insights and turn them into actionable consumer-centric results that resonate with the product’s audience. I am naturally curious, adaptable, sympathetic, and analytical. Years in the hospitality industry helped develop my open-mindedness and empathy and I love to learn about the world and from those around me.

My Skills

USER RESEARCH I start by creating a well-defined goal. It is important to be focused and relevant to the situation I am trying to understand. Research tools need to be practical with the resources at hand as well as functional. QUALITATIVE INTERVIEWS My approach is to create an interview guide with open-ended questions, use active listening techniques, look for body language cues while acknowledging emotions and help the user feel comfortable in order to obtain accurate feedback. USER EMPATHY I use my ability to understand others perspectives from their point of view while creating, conducting testing analyzing results, and ideating potential solutions.
